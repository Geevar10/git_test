Spaghetti Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add garlic and cook until fragrant.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Toss the cooked spaghetti with the tomato sauce and basil.
