Meatballs Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine ground beef, breadcrumbs, egg, garlic, and milk. Mix well.
  2. Form the mixture into meatballs.
  3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add meatballs and cook until browned on all sides.
  4. Reduce heat and simmer for 10-15 minutes, or until cooked through.
